## Taxonomy

- *Velella* and *Porpita* are no longer considered siphonophores. They are
  hydrozoans in the clade Capitata. Older literature (and many records in
  this corpus) place them within Siphonophora; treat such placements as
  historical and reflect current consensus when summarizing.
